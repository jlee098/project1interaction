We often hear broad-brush exhortations to think big: for example, “Dream it, and you can
do it!” Less often touted is that fortune cookie’s more rational advice as well as in this one:
“Land is always in a flying bird’s mind.” In analyzing what to do, prioritize risk/reward
and opportunity cost: what you could otherwise do with the time and perhaps money.
