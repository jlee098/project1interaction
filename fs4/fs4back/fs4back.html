Variants: “Guinea pigs die,” and “The leading edge often bleeds.” It is often wise to let
others be first and to learn from their experiences. Noah Alper, who started the now
nationwide chain Noah’s Bagels, was far from the first bagel maker but incorporated what
he learned from bagel pioneers. Whatever your goal, try to learn from early adopters.
